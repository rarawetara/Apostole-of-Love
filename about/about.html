COMPOSER ANATOLII DOLESKO

Born 7 January 1972 in Chernihiv (Ukraine).

Over the past ten years, the author has created a large-scale work, which is the Neoclassical Oratorio "The Apostle of Love" for electric guitar solo, mixed choir, soloists, rock band and a large symphony orchestra.

The author assigned a special role in writing the work to the main guitar part, which he performs personally.
